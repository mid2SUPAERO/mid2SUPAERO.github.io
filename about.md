# About

I am an enseignant-chercheur (professor) at **ISAE-SUPAERO**, in the DMSM department
(Département Mécanique des Structures et Matériaux), attached to the research group
**Modélisation des systèmes et microsystèmes mécaniques (DMSM/MS2M)**.

I lead the **MID2** group — *Multidisciplinary optimization for aerospace Innovation:
eco Design and Data* — which develops methods for structural optimization,
system identification, and data-driven/AI-assisted decision-making, with applications
in aerospace engineering and complex dynamical systems.

## Scientific themes

- Conception et simulation conditionnées par des exigences multiphysiques (DMSM)
- Interactions fluides–structures (DISC)
- Optimisation et analyse multidisciplinaire (DMSM)
